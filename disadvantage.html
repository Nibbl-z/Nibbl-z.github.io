Disadvantages

  • May cause unexpected allergic reactions: GM crops contain DNA from other organisms, so it may trigger allergic reactions.
  • May increase antibiotic resistance: GMO crops make the crops resistant to antibiotics, but sometimes the antibiotic resistance gets spread to the consumer.
  • May have an increased production of toxins: GMO crops sometimes make more toxins than normal plants, making it harmful to humans.
