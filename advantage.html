Advantages

  • Higher yields: Crops will produce more
  • Longer shelf life: Crops will still be ok for purchase for longer
  • Resistant to diseases and pests: Makes growing crops a lot easier, because disease and pests will not damage the crops
  • Has better taste: GMO crops restore some of the flavor that normal crops lose
  • Grows faster: GMO crops can increase the amount of sunlight energy crop plants can put into food production.
  • More nutritious: Some GMO crops have been modified to have more nutritional value.
  • Cheaper to buy: Because less water, land, and pesticides are used, producing GMO crops is cheaper.
